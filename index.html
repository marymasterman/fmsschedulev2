Faculty roster & goals

Add or edit faculty here. Check which services each person does, set their goals for this 6-month period, and choose their weekend-combination rule.

Weekend-combo rules — what each option means
  • Solo only — this person covers only one service on a weekend. This is the default.
  • Gold + OB ok — can cover both Gold and OB on the same weekend (e.g., AMP, SNG).

Hard global rule: OB and Bronze are never scheduled together on the same weekend. All other combinations are considered violations unless explicitly allowed above.

Rules being checked (click to see)
  • Faculty can only be assigned to services they're eligible for.
  • Gold weekend is always the same person as Gold week (no separate Gold weekend assignment).
  • OB and Bronze weekends prefer the same person who's on that service that week — only fall back to someone else if there's a hard-block weekend, weekend combo-rule violation, or the global OB+Bronze weekend exclusion.
  • Hard-block week/weekend requests are violations if assigned.
  • No back-to-back inpatient weeks.
  • Same person cannot do two services in the same week.
  • OB and Bronze cannot be covered by one person on the same weekend.
  • Faculty weekend-combo rule must be honored (e.g., "solo only" can't cover two services on one weekend).
  • Holiday repeats — faculty who worked a major holiday shouldn't get another major holiday in this period.
  • Soft avoids and ~every-6-weeks cadence shown as soft flags, not hard blocks.
